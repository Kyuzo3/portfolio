About Me

I like challenging myself to find and solve the source of flaws in the design of games. I am constantly improving my coding and design skills, making sure to be better than yesterday. As a gamer, I am highly skilled in PvP games. Usually in the Top 0.1% - 5% ranking in Mobas, Shooters, and Fighters. I also enjoy unlocking every achievement, collecting transmogs, hunting shiny pokemon, clearing every side quest, and defeating valkyries. To me, games are great works of art. many game companies have begun to undermine their brands to make more money from their games. This makes it difficult to find good games. That's why I decided to make my games. I want to show people that money can be made without producing hollow, fabricated games. I want to bring together a community of passionate gamers like myself and deliver beautiful artwork to them.
